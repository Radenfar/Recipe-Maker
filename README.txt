Recipe Maker
----------------
No dependencies - just unzip and run app.py!

How to use:
1 - Open ingredients.txt and note down which ingredients you have at your disposal such as shown in the file upon download.
2 - Run the app.py
3 - Click enter to continue to generate random recipes for you to try!

Warning:
You may have to click a few times to get a genuinely good recipe as I do not support food waste, however this program can invent some great dishes!
